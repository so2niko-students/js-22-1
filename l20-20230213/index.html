Lockwood & Co. (TV series)

Lockwood & Co. is a British detective thriller television series developed by Joe Cornish for Netflix based on Jonathan Stroud's book series of the same name. Consisting of eight episodes, it premiered on 27 January 2023.[1] It follows the plots of the first two books, The Screaming Staircase and The Whispering Skull.[2]
